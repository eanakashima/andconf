Welcome to &:conf

&:conf, pronounced "and conf," is a code retreat and an unconference for intersectional feminist web developers, August 14-17, 2015 in the woods of Western Sonoma County. Saturday’s code retreat will be a day of pair programming on one kata where each hour we throw our code away and work with a new person. Sunday’s unconference will have two tracks: yak and hack, where we'll talk about technologies, programming, careers, and culture.

&:conf will take place at St. Dorothy's Rest, a retreat center in Occidental, CA.

Code of Conduct

&:conf is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, age or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. Conference participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the conference without a refund at the discretion of the conference organizers.

Our full code of conduct can be found at: Code of Conduct
